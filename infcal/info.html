Infdev California

Experience the insanity of California in wildfire season in inf-20100630-1. Features: -Wildfires are frequent and varying levels of smog cover the atmosphere. -Basically everything you can do in Minecraft is locked behind a permit, and you have to get $1 million in-game to restore all functionality. -This is achieved by killing animals and monsters, and buying and renting out different properties which range from literal rocks to city skyscrapers. -In order to get all of the permits you have to buy them at a permit office building. -Freeways and cities and suburbs that can surround them in places. -6 Biomes based on California: Normal, Desert, Joshua Tree, Pine Forest, Chaparral, Redwood Forest. -A seecret feature.
